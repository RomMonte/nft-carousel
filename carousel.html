From photography to magic

These visual compositions were created through the digital processing of photographs.

Magic is found in creation itself, in the details, colours, associations, symmetry, and image… In the realization of the simplicity of creating new visual worlds and the infinite possibilities of combining forms…

This collection does not depict darkness. It unveils the shadows residing in the viewer’s perception.
